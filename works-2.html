Project 60DAYS 2017 - Bring global classrooms to children on Ly Son Island, Vietnam

My role in this project are Human Resources & Content Associate.

Project 60DAYS provided a 2-month-learning program for 135 high school students on Lyson Island, Vietnam, from June to August 2017, in order to help our students prepare for the College Entrance Exam. Projects 60DAYS also organized workshops about soft skills, music contests, and team-building games.

My contribution as a Human Resources & Content Associate:

My team has recruited 13 talented volunteers from more than 600 applicants through 3 rounds: online application, academic tests, and interviews. I also led the team to build detailed plans for teaching activities, skill-development activities, living, transportation, and logistics for a 2-month project.
